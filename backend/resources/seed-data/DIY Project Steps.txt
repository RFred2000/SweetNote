Gather materials - Paint (acrylic), brushes (assorted sizes), canvas (16"x20")
Sketch design outline - Use pencil to lightly sketch the composition and main elements of the painting
Paint base layers - Apply background colors and basic shapes to establish the foundation of the artwork
Add details and finishing touches - Gradually add layers of paint to add depth and dimension, focusing on highlights and shadows
Let dry and display - Allow the painting to dry completely before framing or hanging it in a prominent place in your home.
